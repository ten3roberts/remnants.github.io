Remnants of a Modern World is a game in a post apocalyptic setting.

Beings of unknown origin have taken over Earth, as it was once called. Their intent is not known, nor is their motive. Yet, they hold the few remaining specimens of humans alive, exposing them to countless of horrific experiments.

Humanity was once a blooming society full of potential; now, none of that remains.

You are the legacy of what once was.
